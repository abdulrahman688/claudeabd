Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

International shipping typically takes 2-4 weeks. We ship from Damascus, Syria, so please allow time for customs clearance in your country.

What makes Aleppo soap special?

Aleppo soap is made using a 1,000-year-old UNESCO-recognized traditional method. It contains only natural ingredients (olive oil and laurel oil), is aged for 6-9 months, and is completely biodegradable. It's gentle, moisturizing, and suitable for all skin types.

How much do the women actually earn?

Our artisans earn €80-90 per month on average, which is significantly above the local average wage. 70% of every purchase goes directly to artisan wages. We believe in radical transparency about our financial model.

Can I visit your workshop?

We welcome visitors when security conditions permit. Please contact us in advance to arrange a visit to our Damascus workshop. You'll meet the artisans, see the traditional soap-making process, and experience Syrian hospitality.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes! We ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs vary by location. Some countries may have restrictions or delays due to sanctions, so please contact us if you have concerns about delivery to your location.

How should I store and use Aleppo soap?

Store in a cool, dry place. Use with water to create a rich lather. One bar lasts 2-3 months with daily use. The soap becomes milder and more luxurious as it ages, so don't worry if it looks dry—that's normal!

Can I buy in bulk or wholesale?

Yes! We offer wholesale rates for retailers, spas, hotels, and bulk buyers. Please contact us at wholesale@zenobiaslegacy.com for pricing and minimum order quantities.

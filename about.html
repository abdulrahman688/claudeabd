About Us

Building dignity through ancient craftsmanship

Our Mission

Zenobia's Legacy exists to empower Syrian widows through meaningful work, preserve 1,000 years of soap-making heritage, and create ethical commerce that puts people before profit.

Empowerment

We provide dignified employment, not charity. Every woman earns a fair wage for skilled work.

Heritage

We preserve UNESCO-recognized traditional soap-making techniques passed down for over 1,000 years.

Ethics

70% of every purchase goes directly to artisan wages. We believe in radical transparency.

Quality

Premium natural ingredients, traditional methods, and patient aging create exceptional soap.

Community

We build connections between Syrian artisans and conscious consumers worldwide.

Sustainability

Biodegradable products, minimal packaging, zero-waste production, and local sourcing.

Why It Matters

The Reality

Syria's conflict has left hundreds of thousands of widows struggling to survive. Many are educated, skilled women who suddenly found themselves the sole provider for their families with limited opportunities.

Traditional soap-making, a UNESCO-recognized Syrian heritage craft, was nearly lost. Master artisans fled or were displaced. Ancient workshops were destroyed.

Cheap, mass-produced soap flooded the market, threatening a craft that had survived empires and millennia.

Our Solution

We train Syrian widows in traditional Aleppo soap-making, providing them with dignified employment that honors their skills and heritage.

By creating global demand for authentic, ethically-made Aleppo soap, we preserve an ancient craft while creating sustainable livelihoods.

Every purchase directly supports a family, sends children to school, and proves that ethical commerce can change lives.

How We Work

1. Recruitment & Training

We identify Syrian widows in need and provide comprehensive training in traditional soap-making from master craftswomen.

2. Fair Employment

Women earn €80-90/month—significantly above local average. They work in safe, respectful conditions with flexible hours.

3. Quality Production

Using traditional methods and premium ingredients, our artisans create soap that meets international quality standards.

4. Ethical Marketing

We tell authentic stories with dignity—celebrating strength, not exploiting hardship. Women control their own narratives.

5. Continuous Support

Beyond wages, we provide community, skills development, and pathways to financial independence.

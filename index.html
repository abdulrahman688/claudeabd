A Legacy of Strength

Queen Zenobia

In 270 CE, Queen Zenobia ruled one of the most powerful empires in the ancient world from Palmyra, Syria. She was known for her wisdom, strength, and independence.

Her Spirit Lives On

Today, 10 Syrian widows in Damascus continue her legacy of strength and independence through traditional Aleppo soap-making, supporting 40 children.

Your Impact

Every bar you buy supports a woman to earn €85/month, feed her family, and send her children to school. You're not buying soap—you're investing in dignity.

UNESCO Heritage

1,000 Years of Tradition

Aleppo soap-making is a UNESCO-recognized craft that has been passed down for over a millennium. Made from pure olive oil and laurel oil, each bar is naturally aged for 6-9 months.

  • 100% Natural Ingredients
  • Handmade by Master Craftswomen
  • Traditional Cold-Process Method
  • 6-9 Months Natural Aging
